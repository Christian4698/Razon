# ADR-003: NO TRADE As A First-Class Result

Date: 2026-06-04
Status: Accepted

## Context

RAZON must protect capital and avoid forcing trades when market conditions,
data quality, or risk constraints are not acceptable.

## Decision

NO TRADE is treated as a valid system output, not as an error. Future analysis,
KALOS, Risk Engine, and execution modules must preserve refusal reasons.

## Consequences

The platform can prioritize survival over activity. Journals and dashboards must
show refused opportunities with enough context for review and improvement.
