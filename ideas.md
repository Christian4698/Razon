# RAZON V1 Foundation

RAZON is an AI Trading Analysis Platform powered by General Tech Consult.

V1 principles:

- API-first analysis workflow.
- Demo market-data simulation only.
- No automatic trading.
- No MT5 connection.
- No unverified performance results.
- Signal decisions are recorded in the decision journal.

Visible modules:

- RAZON Dashboard
- RAZON Signals
- RAZON Analysis
- RAZON Positions
- RAZON Protect
- RAZON Analytics
- RAZON Settings
